# Open-Price

**Open-Price** is a privacy-first, consumer-focused application that enables people to look up and share real-world product pricing by location‚Äîwithout surveillance, profiling, or exploitation.

The project exists to combat opaque pricing practices such as surveillance pricing, algorithmic price discrimination, and other predatory behaviors that disadvantage consumers.

No ads.  
No trackers.  
No required accounts.

Just real prices, reported by real people.

---

## Why This Project Exists

Consumers increasingly face:
- Location-based price manipulation
- Surveillance pricing driven by personal data
- Price comparison tools funded by ads and affiliates
- Lack of transparency around in-store pricing

Open-Price is designed to reverse that power imbalance by providing a community-driven, privacy-respecting alternative.

## What the App Does

Users can look up and submit product pricing using:
- Address
- City
- State
- ZIP code

Each price report may include:
- Product name or barcode
- Price
- Store name
- Store address and phone number
- Approximate location (opt-in)
- Timestamp and age of report
- Optional photo or barcode verification

---

## Feature Roadmap

### MVP (Must Have)
- Product search (barcode + text)
- Manual and scanned price submission
- Photo upload for verification
- Store and location tagging (opt-in, coarse by default)
- Price history per product
- Community validation (confirm / flag)
- Full functionality without account creation

### Power User Features
- Contributor price accuracy scores
- Store price heatmaps
- Alerts for price drops
- Best nearby price finder
- Offline price logging with later sync
- Household staples watchlists

### Advanced Features
- Receipt scanning with automatic item extraction
- AI-assisted fraud and anomaly detection
- Predictive pricing trends (non-personalized)
- Store price reliability and volatility scores

---

## Trust & Validation Model

- Multiple confirmations increase confidence
- Photos and barcode scans increase verification weight
- Disputed prices are clearly labeled
- Reputation is based on accuracy, not identity
- No hidden scoring or shadow moderation
