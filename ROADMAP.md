# Open-Price Roadmap

This roadmap outlines high-level phases and guiding priorities for Open-Price.
It is intentionally non-exhaustive and subject to public discussion and revision.

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Complete)

- Founding principles and governance
- Privacy guarantees and transparency commitments
- Open-source licensing
- Contributor guidelines
- Cryptographic proof of authorship and timing

---

## Phase 2 — MVP Definition & Risk Modeling

- Define core data models (products, prices, locations)
- Privacy and threat modeling
- Abuse and fraud considerations
- MVP feature prioritization
- Community feedback and review

---

## Phase 3 — MVP Implementation

- Product search (barcode and text)
- Price submission with verification
- Store and coarse location tagging
- Price history per item
- Community validation mechanisms

---

## Phase 4 — Pilot & Iteration

- Limited real-world usage
- Data quality assessment
- UX improvements
- Performance and scalability review

---

## Explicit Non-Goals

- Advertising or surveillance-based monetization
- Individualized or manipulative pricing
- User profiling or behavioral tracking
- Closed or proprietary data systems

---

## Guiding Principles

- Privacy by default
- Transparency over growth
- Accuracy over volume
- Users over institutions
